Spring 2017 First Meeting

Come see what we do!

What

Join us for our first meeting to learn more about CU GameDev! We are excited for this upcoming semester to get Columbia students to learn more about how we design, create and, of course, play games. We host game jams, offer game development workshops, and invite experienced game designers to teach us about the possibilities in game development. Students from all years and levels of experience welcome. Bring your friends, learn about games and have some fun!

There will be pizza!

If you don't have housing through Columbia and need to be signed in, let us know either by email or through our Facebook event.
