Unity Workshop:

Game Development in Unity3D Continued

In collaboration with Columbia ACM!

What

This workshop, led by Luis Tolosa (SEAS, 2016) will teach you how to create this augmented version of Unity's Roll-A-Ball demo from scratch. We will cover the basics of Unity's interface and provide an introduction to scripting in C# for Unity. Open to beginning and experienced programmers, this workshop is designed to help people of all skill levels get started making video games!

Where

603 Hamilton Hall

When

Monday, October 5th from 8:00pm - 9:00pm.
