Chinatown Fair Arcade Trip

Because leaving the Morningside bubble is a thing.

What

A bonding event to welcome new members into the club.

Where

Joe's Shanghai (for food) and the Chinatown Fair Arcade (for games).

When

Friday, February 27th from 8:00pm - 11:00pm.

Info

The Chinatown Fair Arcade is known for its old-school arcade feel, though its collection has grown from classics like Marvel vs Capcom and Street Fight to DDR, any number of rhythm games, and some Japanese arcade games that you'd be hard-pressed to find elsewhere in the United States. As for Joe's Shanghai? It's some of the dankest food in Chinatown. And that's saying something.
